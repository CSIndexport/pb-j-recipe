Banana Smoothie Recipie

Learn How to Make a Banana Smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 1 sliced banana
  • 1/4 cup of Greek Yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Slice up the banana
  2. one by one, In the blender cup, mix 1/4 cup of greek yogurt, 1/4 cup of milk and 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  3. Put the sliced bananas into the blender, and start blending ALL the ingredients together!
